😀

 The classic smiley face emoji. When recieved from a younger person it could mean less interest in the conversation. When recieved from an older person it could mean great joy or happiness. Not usable with younger generations, usually reserved for older folks. Using this emoji will date your texting skills. 5/10

😎

 The Cool Emoji- he's got sunglasses on! This emoji is best used for any age range- it's adored by young and old alike- and is a true indicator of "cool factor." Combine with 2 pointing emojis for added effect. Extemley high usablility, whether showing a cool project you completed, feeling good about something or simply for indicating how bright it is outside. None 10/10

😂

 Laughing crying emoji This emoji is targeted towards younger audiences but is becoming more adopted by all. Good for using as a funny reaction to a funny story/meme, but be careful about using too many, as that might come across as sarcastic. Can only use a few in a row before it becomes too much, 3 is a good max per message. 7/10

😳

 Flushed Face Emoji The target audience for this emoji is hard to guage, probably a younger, more edgy crowd. This emoji is more niche, although when used correctly it can be quite humorous. Good for sarcastic reactions or reactions to the most outlandish thing's you've ever heard. If used on accident can make your recipent feel embarrassed for whatever they just said, causing confusion. 8/10

🤓

 Nerd Glasses Emoji A recently trending meme emoji that's exploded with younger generations. Used for sarcasm or when someone is talking a little too passionately about something. Can cause major harm to one's self-confidence when used in a cruel manner. Use carefully 8/10

😩

 Weary Emoji Bro I don't know who made this emoji and why but it was a horrible idea. Use if you want to make any conversation awkward and make your friends question your sanity Don't use. 2/10

😭

 Crying Emoji Good for any age group, a universal sign of grief or appologetics. Good for expressing sorrow, in an appology, showing empathy, overall great reaction emoji. Using too many may come across as insincere, never use more than 5 at a time. 9/10
